# Human Resources Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project was developed as part of the **CodeAlpha Data Analytics Internship**. It features an interactive **Human Resources Analytics Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Power BI** using the IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition dataset.

The dashboard provides insights into employee attrition, job satisfaction, performance, overtime, and workforce distribution to support data-driven HR decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition across departments and job roles.
- Monitor key HR metrics through interactive KPI cards.
- Evaluate job satisfaction and performance trends.
- Examine the relationship between overtime and employee turnover.
- Support HR planning and workforce management with interactive visualizations.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards:
  - Total Employees
  - Attrition Count
  - Attrition Rate (%)
  - Average Job Satisfaction
  - Average Performance Rating

- Interactive Visualizations:
  - Attrition (Yes vs No)
  - Attrition by Department
  - Attrition by Job Role
  - Overtime vs Attrition
  - Average Job Satisfaction by Department
  - Employee Count by Department
  - Average Monthly Income by Job Role

- Slicers for:
  - Department
  - Job Role
  - Gender
  - Marital Status
  - Education Field
  - Overtime

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Identified departments and job roles with higher attrition levels.
- Compared employees who worked overtime with attrition outcomes.
- Measured average job satisfaction across departments.
- Analyzed workforce distribution by department.
- Explored salary differences across job roles using average monthly income.
